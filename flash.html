Affer that fight
In the aftermath of the dragon's destruction, the people of the kingdom came together with a determination to rebuild what they had lost. Led by a council of wise elders and skilled craftsmen, they toiled tirelessly to erect new homes, restore the once magnificent castle, and cultivate the barren lands.
With each passing year, signs of progress emerged. Fields once barren now bore lush crops, and bustling villages sprung up where ruins had once stood. The scars of the past were slowly fading, replaced by a renewed sense of purpose and resilience among the people.
Yet, the shadow of the dragon still loomed large in the hearts of many. Fear lingered in the whispers of the wind, and tales of the beast's ferocity were passed down from generation to generation like cautionary legends.
And so, ten years after the dragon's destruction, the kingdom stood stronger than ever before. Though scars of the past remained, they served as a reminder of the resilience and bravery of its people. And as the sun set on the horizon, casting golden hues across the land, a new era of peace and prosperity dawned upon the once-ruined kingdom.
